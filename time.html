Time Traveler

Category

Date

This film is inspired by Barett Newman's work "Man, heoric and sublime".

In the film Time Traveler, I was trying to explore the boundaries of time and space, and the relationship between each other. The first part of the film is more realistic. It expresses the anxiety and danger, which corresponds to my first impression of Barnett Newman's painting Man, heroic and sublime. Then in the second part of the film, the emotion shifts to deep sadness, which is also inspired by the painting. The second part is more vague and surreal, "traveling" through different themes, such as flower, rain, ocean, religion, etc. In the end, the whole film reverses back to the beginning. As indicated by the title "Time Traveler", viewers are traveling through time and space, but finally, have to go back to reality.
